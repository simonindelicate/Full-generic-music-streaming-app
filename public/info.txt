***What is this website?***

This website is designed to allow you to stream most of Simon Indelicate's solo catalogue.



***How do I use it?***


Press play. 

Use the track skip controls or swipe left and right to skip tracks.

Use the tracklist button to pick a track from the current album.

Use the list of albums covers to choose a different album.

On desktop, toggle the more albums menu with the albums button.

Use the copy link to clipboard button and paste the formatted link in your socials to share the track directly.



***Why Does it exist?***


Several reasons.

1. I think it's nice to sit down and focus on a single album. This site provides a way to do this digitally with the convenience of streaming but without the distractions of a streaming app.

2. I wanted to put all these records in one place.

3. I wanted a better way to share individual tracks.

4. I am very bored of the convention that says that if I want to make music available to you I have to pay rent to a platform landlord owned by a megacorporation. Bandcamp, Spotify, YouTube, Distributors - all these centralised quasi monopolies in an inevitable spiral of enshittification. If I'm not going to get paid anything worth having for streaming, I may as well stream on my terms in a space I control.
